Billing Questions

How do I view my billing history?

To view your billing history, follow these steps:

  1. Log into your WatchWave account.
  2. Navigate to the "Billing" section.
  3. Select "Billing History" to view your past invoices and payments.

How do I update my payment method?

To update your payment method, please follow these steps:

  1. Log into your WatchWave account.
  2. Go to the "Billing" section.
  3. Select "Payment Methods" and add a new payment method.
  4. Remove the old payment method if it's no longer in use.
