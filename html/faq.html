WatchWave FAQ

1. What is WatchWave?
WatchWave is a website that provides the latest information and reviews on popular movies from various genres and countries.
2. How do I search for movies on WatchWave?
You can use the search feature at the top of the page to find the movies you are looking for by title, genre, or actor.
3. Does WatchWave offer movie streaming?
Currently, WatchWave does not offer movie streaming services. We only provide information and reviews about movies.
4. How do I add a movie to my favorites?
To add a movie to your favorites, you need to sign in to your WatchWave account. On the movie page, click the "Add to Favorites" button, and the movie will be saved to your favorites list.
5. How can I contact the WatchWave support team?
You can contact our support team through the 'Contact Us' page available at the bottom of the site.
