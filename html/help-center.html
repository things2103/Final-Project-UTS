Account & Settings

Managing your WatchWave account is easy. Here are some topics you might find helpful:

  • How to Change Your Password: Go to the account settings page and select "Change Password".
  • Updating Your Email Address: In your profile settings, enter your new email address and confirm it.
  • Managing Profiles: Create, edit, or delete profiles to personalize your experience.

Content & Playback

Learn how to find and enjoy content on WatchWave:

  • Searching for Movies and Shows: Use the search bar to find your favorite titles.
  • Using Watchlists: Add movies and shows to your watchlist for easy access later.
  • Adjusting Playback Settings: Change subtitles, audio language, and video quality in the playback settings.
